Hi, welcome to my homepage! I'm a second-year PhD student in Computer Science at the University of California, San Diego, advised by [Prof. Prithviraj (Raj) Ammanabrolu](https://prithvirajva.com/) at [Pearls Lab](https://pearls-lab.github.io/)ðŸ§‹.

My research focuses on **trustworthy AI for language models and agents**, with an emphasis on **[pluralistic alignment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.01167)**, **[reasoning](https://aclanthology.org/2025.emnlp-main.152.pdf)**, and **[interpretability](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=FaOeBrlPst)**. I develop methods that help systems <u>infer user intent through interaction</u>, formalize it into clear, scalable evaluation criteria, and use these signals to <u>train and assess</u> agents on complex, long horizon tasks where success is <u>difficult to verify</u>. I also study **reinforcement learning** for adaptive multi-agent systems that support effective human AI collaboration while respecting diverse values and constraints. My long term goal is to build **safe, controllable agentic systems** that reliably accomplish economically valuable work, and to understand their social and ethical implications in real decision-making.
